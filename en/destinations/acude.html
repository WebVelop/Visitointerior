ABOUT THE DESTINATION

A hidden retreat in the heart of inland Portugal.

Located in Ervedal da Beira, in the municipality of Oliveira do Hospital, Açude da Ribeira is a place that invites visitors to enjoy moments of relaxation and leisure by the river. The weir keeps the waters calm, while the riverbanks provide the perfect setting to unwind or take a peaceful walk. A modern wooden boardwalk allows visitors to comfortably explore the riverside, getting closer to the water and appreciating the landscape from different perspectives. Açude da Ribeira is a place where tranquillity takes centre stage, ideal for discovering the beauty of the surroundings and enjoying time immersed in nature.
