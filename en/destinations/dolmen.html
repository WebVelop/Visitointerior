ABOUT THE DESTINATION

A journey into Portugal's ancient past.

In Fiais da Telha, a small village in the parish of Oliveira do Conde, within the municipality of Carregal do Sal, stands the Dolmen da Orca, a remarkable megalithic monument that impresses visitors with its history and silent presence in the surrounding landscape. Also known as Lapa da Orca or Orca de Fiais da Telha, this ancient structure combines the simplicity of stone with the grandeur of thousands of years of history. Classified as a National Monument, the dolmen features a polygonal stone chamber that serves as a reminder of Portugal's prehistoric past. Visiting the Dolmen da Orca is an invitation to explore, reflect and discover a hidden treasure that continues to captivate everyone who encounters it.
