LEGAL INFORMATION

Protecting your personal data is important to VisitoInterior. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use and protect information from users of our website.

1. Data Controller

The VisitoInterior website is responsible for the processing of personal data collected through this website, in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

2. Data Collected

We may collect information voluntarily provided by users, namely through contact forms, newsletter subscriptions or other means made available on the website.

Technical data may also be collected, such as IP address, browser type, device used and statistical browsing information.

3. Purpose of Data Collection

The data collected is used exclusively to:

  • Respond to contact requests;
  • Improve the browsing experience;
  • Send information requested by the user;
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations.

4. Data Retention

Personal data will only be retained for the period necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected or to comply with legal obligations.

5. Data Sharing

VisitoInterior does not sell or transfer personal data to third parties, except where required by law or necessary for the provision of services related to the operation of the website.

6. Cookies

This website may use cookies to improve performance and browsing experience. Users may configure their browser to refuse or delete cookies at any time.

7. User Rights

Under applicable legislation, users have the right to access, correct, update, restrict or delete their personal data, as well as withdraw previously granted consent.

8. Security

Appropriate technical and organisational measures are implemented to protect personal data against loss, misuse, unauthorised access or disclosure.

9. Changes to this Policy

VisitoInterior may update this Privacy Policy whenever necessary. Users are encouraged to review this page periodically.

10. Contact

For any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or the processing of personal data, you may contact us through the means provided on the Contact page.
